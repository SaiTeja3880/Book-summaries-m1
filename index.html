The Book in Three Sentences:

Rich Dad Poor Dad is a book about the difference between two types of fathers, one who is rich and one who is poor. The author, Robert Kiyosaki, shares his experiences growing up with two fathers, his biological father who was highly educated but financially struggling and his best friend's father who was wealthy but never graduated from high school. The book emphasizes the importance of financial education and encourages readers to change their mindset about money. It teaches the importance of having multiple streams of income, investing in assets, and avoiding debt.
